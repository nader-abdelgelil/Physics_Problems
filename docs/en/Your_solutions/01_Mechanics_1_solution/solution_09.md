# Momentum Comparison

## Problem
Compare momentum of:
- Fly: mass = 2 g, velocity = 10 m/s
- Tennis ball: mass = 60 g, velocity = 1 m/s

---

## Step 1: Convert units

Fly:
m = 2 g = 0.002 kg

Tennis ball:
m = 60 g = 0.06 kg

---

## Step 2: Calculate momentum

Fly:
p = mv = 0.002 × 10 = 0.02 kg·m/s

Tennis ball:
p = mv = 0.06 × 1 = 0.06 kg·m/s

---

## Step 3: Compare

Fly momentum = 0.02  
Tennis ball momentum = 0.06

---

## Final Answer

The tennis ball has greater momentum.
