# Projectile Motion – Full Analysis

## Problem
A projectile is launched with:
- Initial velocity: 100 m/s
- Angle: 37°
- No air resistance

Find:
1. Equations of motion
2. Time of flight
3. Maximum height
4. Range

---

## Step 1: Resolve velocity components

vx = v0 cos(θ) = 100 cos(37°) ≈ 100 × 0.8 = 80 m/s  
vy = v0 sin(θ) = 100 sin(37°) ≈ 100 × 0.6 = 60 m/s  

---

## Step 2: Differential equations

Horizontal motion:
d²x/dt² = 0 → velocity constant

Vertical motion:
d²y/dt² = -g

---

## Step 3: Equations of motion

x(t) = 80t  
y(t) = 60t - (1/2)gt²  

Take g ≈ 10 m/s²:

y(t) = 60t - 5t²

---

## Step 4: Time of flight

At landing: y = 0

60t - 5t² = 0  
t(60 - 5t) = 0  

t = 12 seconds

---

## Step 5: Maximum height

At peak: vy = 0

vy = 60 - 10t = 0  
t = 6 seconds

Substitute:

y_max = 60(6) - 5(6²)  
y_max = 360 - 180 = 180 m

---

## Step 6: Range

Range = x(t_final)

R = 80 × 12 = 960 m

---

## Final Answers

- Time of flight = 12 s  
- Maximum height = 180 m  
- Range = 960 m
