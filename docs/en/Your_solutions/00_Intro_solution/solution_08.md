# Definite Integrals – Area Under Curve

## Problem
Calculate the area under the curve:

f(x) = sin(x)

from x = 0 to x = π

---

## Step 1: Use the definite integral

Area = ∫₀^π sin(x) dx

---

## Step 2: Find the antiderivative

∫ sin(x) dx = -cos(x)

---

## Step 3: Apply limits

[-cos(x)] from 0 to π

= [-cos(π)] - [-cos(0)]

---

## Step 4: Evaluate values

cos(π) = -1  
cos(0) = 1  

So:

= -(-1) - ( -1 )  
= 1 + 1  
= 2

---

## Final Answer

The area under the curve is:

2
