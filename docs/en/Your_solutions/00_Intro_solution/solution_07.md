# Logic & Series – Fly and Bicycle Problem

## Problem
A bicycle is 10 meters from a wall and moves toward it at 1 m/s.  
A fly starts from the bicycle and flies at 2 m/s back and forth between the bicycle and the wall.

Find the total distance the fly travels before being crushed.

---

## Step 1: Time until collision

The bicycle moves at 1 m/s and needs to cover 10 meters:

Time = Distance / Speed = 10 / 1 = 10 seconds

---

## Step 2: Fly movement

The fly moves continuously for the entire duration (10 seconds) at 2 m/s.

---

## Step 3: Total distance

Distance = Speed × Time = 2 × 10 = 20 meters

---

## Final Answer

The fly travels a total distance of:

20 meters
