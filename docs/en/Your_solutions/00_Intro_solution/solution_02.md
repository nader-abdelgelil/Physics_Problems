# Systems of Equations Solution

## Problem
Solve the following system of equations:

\[
2x + 3y = 12
\]
\[
x - y = 1
\]

---

## Step 1: Express one variable

From the second equation:

\[
x - y = 1 \Rightarrow x = y + 1
\]

---

## Step 2: Substitute into the first equation

\[
2(y + 1) + 3y = 12
\]

---

## Step 3: Simplify

\[
2y + 2 + 3y = 12
\]

\[
5y + 2 = 12
\]

---

## Step 4: Solve for y

\[
5y = 10 \Rightarrow y = 2
\]

---

## Step 5: Find x

\[
x = y + 1 = 3
\]

---

## Final Answer

\[
x = 3, \quad y = 2
\]

---

## Verification

Substitute into original equations:

- \(2(3) + 3(2) = 6 + 6 = 12\) ✅  
- \(3 - 2 = 1\) ✅
